Pancakes BABY!

Ingredients Required

  • 3 Egg whites
  • 1 Whole egg
  • 2 Medium bananas
  • 1 Teaspoon baking powder
  • Vanilla flavouring
  • 1 Scoop vanilla protein powder

Preperation Method

  1. Seperate 3 egg whites (disposing of or saving the yolks)
  2. Place 3 egg whites plus 1 whole egg into blender
  3. Add 2 medium bananas to blender
  4. Add 1 scoop of protein powder (vanilla)
  5. Add a splash of vanilla flavouring
  6. Add 1 teaspoon baking powder
  7. Blend until mixture is smooth and to your desired consistency
  8. Pour batter into lightly greased non-stick pan on medium heat, cooking until bubble form on top of the pancake, flip and repeat
  9. Enjoy with suger-free maple syrup and Kenny's low cal protein ice cream
